SWASH COMPETITION APP

Improve Students behaviour by using digital platforms

SWASH competition App. Through the app, students from across Tanzania get into SWASH(School Water, Sanitation and Hygiene) clubs, perform WASH related activities and share pictures on the app. You the user then gets to vote for the best school and share your views. Through this competition we hope to effect positive behaviour change among students with regards to swash

I have been the trainer and the organizer of this competition during it's second phase at the ILALA district at Dar es Salaam

SWASH APP is based to improve the students behaviours through school competition which it showed the success where by after the competition the schools are still doing the same practises to have clean sanitations
